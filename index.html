Hi, I’m Carl

I love to design high quality digital products.

During the last 8 years I’ve been driving business for startups and larger well-known companies through design.

At the moment I’m working with the AI company Pluto in Copenhagen, to reshape urban asset management for municipalities.

Here are a couple of examples of what I’ve been part of designing with great people during the years:

An Online Employee Engagement Platform with the world's largest network of org charts.

A Virtual Golf Simulator Interface that is revolutionising the world of indoor golf with radar technology.

A bike rental platform in Europe that has had more than 1.000.000 rides where I redesigned the app.

A White-label News App for TV2 local news in Denmark that covers all 5 regions in the country.

Electrical charging and parking features for the app of Denmarks largest energy company.

Before going into product design I was an art director at Mother New York where I worked on clients such as Google, CB2, and The Home Depot.

I’m a Hyper Island graduate in Interactive Art Direction, and I have a BA in Creative Communication from DMJX.
